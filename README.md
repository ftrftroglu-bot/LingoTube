# LingoTube 🎓

**LingoTube** is the ultimate language learning companion for your Mac and browser. It transforms any YouTube video into a powerful, interactive language learning tool.

## 🚀 Key Features

*   **🎬 Dual Subtitles:** Watch videos with both your target language and native language subtitles simultaneously.
*   **📖 Smart Dictionary:** Click on any word in the subtitles to instantly see its translation, definition, and grammar rules.
*   **🧠 Vocabulary Tracker:** Save words with one click and review them later using our interactive spaced-repetition flashcard system.
*   **📊 Dashboard & Progress:** Track your daily learning time, streak, and language proficiency level.
*   **🌍 Multi-Language Support:** Learn English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, and many more!

## 📥 Download
